Hello everybody, i am

Neil Degrasse Tyson

Astrophysicist and writer

I am director of the Hayden Planetarium at the Rose Center for Earth and Space and an associate researcher in the astrophysics department at the American Museum of Natural History. From 2006 to 2011 I was presenter of the science education television program NOVA scienceNOW, on the PBS television channel. In 2014 I presented the Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey series.
